Events

port hope fair upcoming event.

cotton candy sclupting

Join us next year as we welcome Jason and Velma, the two lovely people behind Cotton Sculpts. A pair that specialize in making fun sculptures out of cotton candy. We plan on having a fun contest to see who will be able to make the best sculpture

Pie-eating Contest

After all of the requests, we have finally decided to have a pie eating contest at the fair! With pies sourced from families all over the town, come down and watch members of the town attempt to eat the most!

Sheep Shearing

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to shear a sheep? Come on down to the fair to get taught by the towns best farmers! Shear the wool and get taught the process that makes the wool into the clothes that we wear from day to day.
