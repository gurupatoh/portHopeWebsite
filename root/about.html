The Port Hope Fair is a celebration of both past and present as you learn about and celebrate our history and the important role Northumberland families played in the agricultural landscape many years ago. Early fairs were similar to farmers’ markets where one could sell produce rather than just exhibit it. Such fairs often were spread over many days – the first day for exhibition of stock and giving of premiums, the second day included a sale of horses, the third day a sale of horned cattle and the fourth day a sale of domestic manufacturers. Shorter fairs were sometimes held in the spring, and in some years a local fair would be replaced with a larger regional event. We welcome everyone to come on down to our annual fair, for lots of family fun! Come down to enjoy learn more about farm animals, get to know your friendly local farmers, and so much more.
